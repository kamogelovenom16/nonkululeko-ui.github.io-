Top 5 Free VPN Apps for Android in 2025

VPN apps keep your browsing private and secure. Here are the best free VPNs to use this year:

  1. ProtonVPN – Unlimited free data, no ads.
  2. Windscribe – 10GB free monthly data with excellent speeds.
  3. Hotspot Shield – Reliable connections and fast performance.
  4. TunnelBear – User-friendly interface, great for beginners.
  5. TLS Tunnel – Popular among Android users, easy to configure.
